Logo rationale
The mark is a document with a folded corner — referencing PDF/policy documents — and three lines inside. The bottom amber line represents the key insight extracted from complexity. The amber "Ur" in the logotype signals the personal, accessible tone. Playfair Display signals authority without coldness.
